### Clarifying Notes on Project Inventory

* **Unrouted Component Screens (Note 1):** 
  - `LandingScene3D.jsx` and `CognitiveCore.jsx` are Three.js Canvas visual backgrounds/sub-components stored inside the `pages` directory. They are included in the screens list because they physically exist in `frontend/src/pages/`, though they are loaded into main pages rather than being directly routed.
  - `SalesTrend.jsx` and `Settings.jsx` and `Analytics.jsx` are unrouted component screens that are left in the pages directory but have been replaced/refactored into `SalesAnalysis.jsx`, `SettingsPage.jsx`, and `AnalyticsPage.jsx` respectively. They are included to guarantee complete testing of all files in the pages folder.
* **Commented-out Admin integration:**
  - `AdminSettings.jsx` corresponds to the integration hub which is currently defined in the pages folder, but its route is commented out in `App.jsx` (`/admin/connectors`).
* **Static Audit Reports (Note 2):**
  - The static `.md` reports in `backend/reports/` represent model and layout audit evaluations. Corresponding `.json` audit value files (`churn_dashboard_audit_values.json`, `inventory_dashboard_audit_values.json`, `inventory_ai_model_audit_values.json`, `inventory_arima_live_values.json`, and `inventory_q_learning_values.json`) are associated data-store logs linked to their respective reports and are tested concurrently with the parent markdown validation step.
